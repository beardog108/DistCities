DistCities

DistCities is a very simple distributed website sharing platform.

Just type or paste in some HTML, click seed, and share!

Your webpage will be available as long as at least 1 person has it open.

Pages are stored in cache, so unseeded pages can often be re-opened.

Disclaimer: This software offers no privacy. Arbitrary people can see the IP you use to seed and access pages. This is a P2P system. Your bandwidth is shared.
